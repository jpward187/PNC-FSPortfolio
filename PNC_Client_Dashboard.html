Connect Google Sheet

This dashboard needs a Google Sheet URL to pull live data. Follow the steps below to connect it.

Step 1: Open your Google Sheet with the PNC project data
Step 2: Click the green Share button (top right) → set General access to "Anyone with the link" → role = Viewer
Step 3: Copy the URL from your browser address bar and paste it below
The URL should look like: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1aBcDeFgHiJk.../edit
Your sheet must have tabs named: Projects, Milestones, Action Items, Critical Dates, Proposals, Activity
